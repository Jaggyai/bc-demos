# Cold Outreach Templates — BC Demo Sites
**Use with No_Website_Leads_Combined.xlsx.** Merge fields map to your columns:
`{{Business Name}}`, `{{Demo URL}}` (column N), `{{City}}`, `{{Category 1}}`, `{{Phone}}`

The whole edge here: you're not asking "want a website?" — you're showing one **already built with their name on it.** Lead with the link every time.

## CONVERSION TIPS

1. **Send in small batches (20-30/day)** from a real inbox — not 1,400 at once (spam filters + Gmail sending limits).
2. **Personalize line 1** when you can (mention a recent Google review, their truck, a project). Takes 20 sec, doubles reply rate.
3. **Track replies in the spreadsheet** — add a "Status" column: Sent / Opened / Replied / Closed.
4. **When they reply interested** → send the pricing deck (Jag_Mangat_Services_Pricing.pptx) or just quote: "$990 one-time or $99/mo all-in."
5. **The close:** offer to put it on their domain + Google Business. That's the value they can't get from a demo link.
6. **Best targets first:** businesses WITH a Google rating but NO website = they care about reputation and are missing the easy win.
