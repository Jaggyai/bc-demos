What We Do

Full-Service Plumbing & Gas Solutions

From a dripping tap to a full repipe, our certified plumbers handle every job with the craftsmanship Prince George homeowners trust.

Emergency Repairs

Burst pipes, major leaks, no hot water — we respond fast, day or night, with no overtime surprises.

Drain Cleaning

Clogged sinks, slow drains, sewer backups. Camera inspection and hydro-jetting available.

Water Heaters

Tank, tankless, heat-pump installs. Service, repair, and replacement of any major brand.

Gas Fitting

Certified gas line installation, repair, and inspection. Furnaces, ranges, BBQs, fireplaces.

Bathroom & Kitchen

Full renovations and fixture upgrades — toilets, faucets, showers, garburators, dishwashers.

Leak Detection

Non-invasive leak location using thermal imaging and acoustic detection. We find it before we cut.
